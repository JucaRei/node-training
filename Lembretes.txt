MIGRATIONS
criar migrations:
yarn sequelize migration:create --name=create-users
rodar migrations:
yarn sequelize db:migrate
defazer a ultima migration:
yarn sequelize db:migrate:undo
defazer todas as migrations:
yarn sequelize db:migrate:undo:all
